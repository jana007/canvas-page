Training

Beginning in Summer 2017, our office will offer a variety of Canvas training sessions for instructors. These sessions will be held at the Office of Distance Learning’s Faculty Development Lab (Suite C-3500 University Center C) and throughout campus by request. We will also provide instructors a quick and easy way to schedule one-on-one consultations with our Instructional Development staff or Technical Support staff for personal walkthroughs.

Canvas Workshop Series

We are actively developing a new workshop series to help instructors fully prepare for the transition to Canvas. Each workshop will be split into two, 2-hour parts. Departmental training requests

If you would like to reserve a time and date for a training session at your department, please contact John Braswell at jbraswell@campus.fsu.edu or 850-645-0469. We can begin scheduling training in May 2017.

We also recommend that you peruse the resources listed on our Get Ready and Resources pages to familiarize yourself with Canvas as much as possible.
